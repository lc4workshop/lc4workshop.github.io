🎯 Workshop Overview

The third iteration of LC4 workshop puts emphasis on understanding content variation across domains, developing cross-lingual approaches to bridge gaps between high and low-resource languages, and exploring multi-modal interactions for better content quantification.

This workshop takes a multi-disciplinary approach, focusing not only on cross-domain, cross-lingual, and cross-modal research, but also motivating their applications in industry and wider society.

🔍 Key Research Questions

Domain Impact
How does change in domain of data impact the overall generated contents and systems?
Knowledge Transfer
How to efficiently transfer knowledge from high-resource to low-resource languages?
Multi-Modal Alignment
How to align cross-modal data using appropriate alignment functions for better content analysis?
Optimal Data Utilization
How to better utilize different languages' data optimally for content analysis?
Real-World Applications
How to develop practical applications of multi-modal data in low-resource languages?
Foundation Models
How to employ foundational models to effectively alleviate various challenges?
